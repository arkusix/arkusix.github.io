The year is 2021 and the current month is July. The world is not the same as it was almost two years ago. Ever the since the outbreak of Covid-19 pandemic, 21st century humans have gone drastic changes in how they live their life. Many if not more than half of humans in their productive ages had lost their sense of purpose in life though some came out with finding new values in their life and even new purpose in life. And I am not one of them.

Before the pandemic, I was working as a barista. I was living the life that I had thought I dreamed. Coffee had become an integral part of my life and I could make money out of making coffee for people. Also, I can just fulfill my desire for a cup of coffee almost everyday in my workplace, the best constant perk it was.
